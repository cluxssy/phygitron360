Product name: PHYGITRON 360 What it is: A B2B SaaS HR platform that unifies candidate sourcing, employee training, skill verification, and workforce deployment into one intelligent system. The tagline is "The 360° Talent Intelligence Platform." Design direction: Light and premium. White base, deep purple (#6C27FF) as the hero color, black for bold headlines. Smooth scroll animations, elements fade and slide up as you scroll. Feels like a Stripe or Linear landing page — serious, confident, beautifully spaced. Sections: 1. Navbar: PHYGITRON 360 logo (bold black + purple geometric icon), Links (Features, How it Works, Modules, Pricing), Login (black link), Get Started (solid purple button). Subtle shadow on scroll. 2. Hero: "The Complete Talent / Intelligence Platform" (Intelligence underlined/purple). Subheading in gray. CTAs: "Get Started Free" (purple), "Watch Demo" (border). Social proof: "Trusted by 200+ organizations". Floating dashboard mockup with purple shadow on right. 3. Stats Bar: Light lavender background (#F3EFFF). Four purple bold stats: 12,000+ Candidates Sourced, 98% Verification Accuracy, 340+ Courses Delivered, 60% Faster Hiring. Count-up animation. 4. Problem: White background. Left: "Your talent tools are scattered. Your data is not." 3 pain points with red X. Right: Fragmented state diagram. 5. Solution: Very light purple background. "One platform. One profile. One truth." Diagram showing 4 boxes connecting to a central "360° Talent Profile". 6. Four Modules: 2x2 grid. Cards with purple left border. Source (blue accent), Forge (amber), Verify (violet), Deploy (green). 7. How It Works: Horizontal 5-step flow with animated dashed purple line. Steps: Source, AI screening, Training, Skill verification, Deployment. 8. Feature Highlights: Alternating layout (Text/Mockup). AI skill graph, Custom journey builder, Live trust dashboard. 9. Testimonials: Light lavender background. 3 white quote cards with purple left borders. 10. CTA: Deep purple background. White headline "Ready to unify your talent pipeline?". White button. 11. Footer: White, thin border. Logo + description. Link columns. "Book a Demo" purple outline button. Animations: Floating hero image, count-up stats, drawing line in flow, hover lifts, fade-in/slide-up on scroll. Tone: Confident, intelligent, modern, Stripe-like.
